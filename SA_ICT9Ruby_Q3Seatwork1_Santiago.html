The Little Prince

Synopsis:


The Little Prince is a honest and lovely tale about friendship, loneliness, sadness, and love. The prince is a young child who travels the universe from planet to planet in search of enlightenment. His home planet is an asteroid. He learns about adults' unpredictable behavior throughout his journey.

Characters:

The Little Prince

He is a young and curious prince who lives on a tiny asteroid and travels through the universe in search of understanding and friendship.

The Aviator

The Aviator is depicted as kind, humble, and introspective, with a childlike wonder and a thirst for knowledge.

The Fox

The fox is a source of guidance for the little prince, teaching him about the true meaning of love, relationships, and life itself.

The Conceited Man

He is depicted as a narcissistic and self-absorbed person who spends all his time admiring himself in a mirror.

The King

He is described as a ruler who commands his subjects to do as he says, without questioning his authority. Despite his absolute power, the king is portrayed as being insecure and dependent on the love and admiration of his subjects

The Rose

The rose is depicted as a delicate and beautiful flower who serves as the main love interest for the Little Prince.

The Businessman

The businessman is a character who is obsessed with counting and measuring things, including stars and roses. He represents the narrow-minded, practical and materialistic view of life that the Little Prince encounters on his travels.

The Snake

The snake is a little serpent who the Little Prince meets on his journey through the stars. The Snake is depicted as being wise and experienced, having lived a long life and having seen many things. The Snake symbolizes the cycle of life and death, and the transformative power of love.

The Mother

The mother of the protagonist is portrayed as a strict and overprotective parent who values practicality and success over imagination and creativity.

The Little Girl

The little girl is a girl introduced in the beginning of the movie. Later on, the girl gets to meet the aviator, his neighbor, and the little prince.
